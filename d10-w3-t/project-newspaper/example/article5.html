An Interview with A. Lal

By R. Han

A. Lal, a Summer Lab student, said that he loved to read the book The Homework Machine because it's about some kids who use homework machines to help them with homework.

If Lal had to go anywhere, he would go to New Jersey to be with his cousin.

"I barely go to see him," Lal said, "so I want to go there because he is one of my favorite cousins."

Lal wants to be an NBA player because he loves basketball. In his spare time Lal practices basketball so he can get better and better. Lal wants to go to Duke college because they have a very good basketball team.

"Once I started knowing about basketball, I practiced it and improved," Lal said. "Now I love basketball and want to be an NBA player."

Lal's favorite basketball ball player is Michael Jordan because he is the best basketball player ever and he was also on the Bulls. Besides basketball Lal also loves to play baseball, tennis, and table tennis because he is really good at it and it's also very fun. Lal love to play soccer on the play ground because he likes to kick the ball.

Lal's favorite movie is The Million Dollar Arm. "It is about two people from India who learn how to play baseball and succeed. It is also based on a true story."

Not only does Lal participate in a lot of sporting activities, he also plays the recorder and the viola for instruments. His favorite subject is math because he is really good at math in school.

The person he admires is his dad because his dad help him many things.
